Committee Officers

Each year a new oumpa committee is elected at our AGM in trinity (summer) term for the following year. The committe is responsible for all aspects of the running of the club including training, competitions and finances.

The Captains are repsonsible for organising training and other people are responsible for orgainising the weekly newsletter and socials. The president and secretary jointly take care of the mass of administritive detail so the rest of us don't have to.
