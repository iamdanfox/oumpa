OUMPA's Training Locations

Most of OUMPA's training takes place on the Iffley Road University Sports Complex. The swimming takes place in the new Rosenblatt Swimming Pool.

The running takes place on the famous Iffley Road Running Track where the 4-minute mile record was first broken. Shooting training happens indoors in the Iffley Road Cricket Schools. This is just off the side of the track in the brown brick building on the right of the picutre.

OUMPA also used the Millfield Pentathlon Training camp in Somerset.
