Links to Other Useful Sites

This page has details to other useful websites from the UK and around the world.

Modern Pentathlon Association of Great Britain

The MPAGB is the national governing body for the Modern Pentathlon. This website is the home of the British Pentathlon. They have details of national competitions and events as well as picture galleries and information on the Olympics.

CUMPC - Cambridge University Modern Pentathlon

Offers details on training, social and competitive events calendar, news, photo gallery and links.

Union Internationale de Pentathlon Modernecom

Recognized by the International Olympic Committee as the world governing body for the sport. Includes news, rules, competitions, results and links.

Modern-Pentathlon.com

This site has information on the history of the Pentathlon and details reguarding scoring the five events.

The Pony Club

The Pony Club is an international voluntary youth organisation for those interested in ponies and riding.

The British Shooting Sports Council

The British Shooting Sports Council is an umbrella organisation representing the major shooting associations in the United Kingdom.

Modern Pentathlon on Wikipedia.org

The Wikipedia entry for Modern Pentathlon.
