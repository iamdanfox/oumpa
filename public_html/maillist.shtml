OUMPA Mailist Information

The oumpa maillist is used for communication between current members usually on a daily basis. So if you want to keep up with what's happening it's vital you're a part of it.

Just follow these simple steps to get yourself on the maillist. Then you'll get details of training, competitions and socials. The other main source of information is the LOON.

Get yourself on the Maillist!

Send an e-mail to oumpa-subscribe@maillist.ox.ac.uk with the message text: subscribe oumpa

2. The Webmaster will send you a confirmation request, and once you reply to it, a message confirming that you are a subscriber.

Get yourself off the maillist!

1. Send an e-mail to oumpa-unsubscribe@maillist.ox.ac.uk

2. The Webmaster will send a confirmation request to your address and remove the address once you reply.
